Center for Patient Self Service Portal (PssP)

 

Please use the Firefox browser to access the Patient Portal. For Mac users, the Safari browser may also be used.

The Patient Portal does not function properly with Internet Explorer, MS Edge, or Chrome.

 

If you forget the ID when asked to enter your ID#, the system will not recognize you. 

 

If you have difficulty registering your Patient Portal account, please call 803/323-2206 to speak with the front desk.

 

FOR EMERGENCIES, DIAL 911
 

We are committed to protecting your personal information. Data that you provide cannot be viewed by anyone else on the Web and is securely maintained by industry standard SSL (secure socket layer) encryption and decryption technology when needed. We do not share your information with anyone else.
